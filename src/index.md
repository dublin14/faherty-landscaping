Ivor Faherty Landscaping has served the Dublin area for over 3 decades. Established in 1994 with the aim of offering a professional approach to landscaping design, creation, and garden maintenance.

Transform your outdoor space with expert landscaping services tailored to your needs. Specialising in timber decking, garden walls, and patios, we create stunning, functional outdoor areas that enhance your home's beauty and value.

Whether you're looking for a complete garden makeover or simple upgrades, our skilled team delivers high-quality craftsmanship. From tree planting to full-scale landscaping projects, we bring your vision to life with precision and care. Let us design a space where you can relax, entertain, and enjoy nature in style.
